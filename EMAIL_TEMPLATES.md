## üìù Email Sending Tips

### Timing
- Send Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM in recipient's timezone
- Avoid Mondays (inbox overload) and Fridays (weekend mode)

### Follow-up
- Wait 2 weeks before follow-up
- Keep follow-up brief: "Just checking if you had a chance to review"
- Don't send more than 1 follow-up

### Etiquette
- Keep emails under 200 words
- Be respectful of their time
- Don't expect immediate responses
- Thank them regardless of response
